Programs

  1. AAKAR - the first step

    This program is meant to familiarize street children and their families with the importance of education through counselling by our staff. Later, they are enrolled in our organization where non-formal teaching methods are used to build a love for learning. They are then admitted into schools and supported through learning centers to prevent dropout.

  2. AHAR APURTI

    To distribute food to the underprivileged, downtrodden and marginalized people, especially children from weaker sections of society.

  3. AVSAR - a chance

    Designed to assist meritorious children of underprivileged classes in getting admission in reputed public schools for quality education under EWS quota.

  5. PARIVARTAN - change of direction

    Aims to bring positive change in the lives of street children by convincing and motivating parents to support education, helping with school admissions and providing financial assistance.

  6. UPHAAR - gift a smile

    This project bridges the gap between street children and others by encouraging donation of clothes, toys, and essentials that children have outgrown.
